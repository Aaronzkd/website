Kaidi Zhang

I'm a MS student at Columbia University, working as a graduate research assiatant in ROAM Lab supervised by Prof. Matei Ciocarlie. My research focuses on robotics, particularly in areas such as robot perception, tactile sensing, manipulation, and machine learning.

Before joining Columbia, I received my Bachelors degree in Mechatronics and Robotic Systems at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University, where I worked with Prof. Yuqing Chen on the development of a two-wheeled self-balancing robot.
